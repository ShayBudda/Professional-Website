Eat it or Yeet it?

Thats the age old question. This is a goofy game that provides an array of images assigned with random prompts. Depending on the situation your answer might differ. You must choose weather or not to eat said undisclosed video game animal, or yeet it which means to throw it away. Pretend that all animals are prepared in such a way that they are edible, so if it looks poisonous you will be ok.
