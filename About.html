What does it mean to see the design in everything? It’s not just a philosophical concept; it’s about appreciating the beauty in everyday life. I believe that everything we know has been designed, and all our designs are derived from existing ones. There are no truly new ideas, just old ones reshaped into something different. Consider this AI-generated photograph. It's a blend of countless works by various artists, yet it emerges as an original piece. This is what I love to do: creating new things from old designs and drawing inspiration from everything around me. Ultimately, all design can be traced back to God, the original designer. Taking inspiration from Him is the most original approach one can take.
